Compact aviation and space-based radar for Earth remote sensing systems

A BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF THE IDEA

The widespread use of unmanned aerial vehicles and spacecraft creates a demand for small-sized sensor devices for remote sensing of the Earth. The use of radar means allows for surveying the Earth's surface regardless of atmospheric conditions and lighting, as well as subsurface surveys.

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Small radar system with antenna system and integrated digital processing

Characteristic Meaning
Operating frequencies 9-10 GHz
Antenna gain ~14 dB
Antenna pattern 15°х30°
Average radiated power up to 80 MW
Peak radiated power up to 1 Watt
Working height range up to 1 km
The width of the shooting band on the ground up to 1 km
Resolution capacity 0,3х0,3 m
The length of the survey strip on the ground at least 20 km
Range of media movement speeds 5-35 m/s
Supply voltage 12 V
Power consumption in radar survey mode not more than 30 watts
Weight (without battery) 900 grams
Sizes 225х85х65
APPLICATION AREAS

High-resolution mapping, creation of digital terrain models and 3D terrain maps, monitoring of ice conditions, analysis of the spread of natural disasters, control of agricultural, forest lands and fires
