Innovative products for effective fire extinguishing (fire extinguishing agent - powder)

In cooperation with LLC "Strada" , we offer innovative products for fire fighting and local fires.

LLC "SME INNOVATIONS" is the official partner for the export and promotion of Strada products in the CIS countries, India, Southeast Asia, North America and Europe.

PURPOSE OF THE DEVICE

Extinguishing classes A,B,C,E

An autonomous powder fire extinguishing device is designed to extinguish fires of solid, liquid, gaseous substances and electrical equipment under voltage without human intervention.

COMPOSITION AND OPERATION OF THE DEVICE

It consists of a foam body filled with fire extinguishing powder. Fire-conducting cords run along the sides of the housing, which activate the device upon contact with fire, a pop is heard within 10 seconds (an additional warning signal about a fire), the housing ruptures and fire extinguishing powder is released, after which the source of ignition will be instantly localized.

ADVANTAGES
  • They are easy to use and do not require any special skills
  • High reaction rate to the occurrence of fire and instant suppression of fire
  • Maintenance-free
  • They are environmentally friendly and do not contain harmful substances
  • Offline and manual operation of the device
  • Safe for others when triggered
  • Noise alert when triggered
  • Shelf life - 10 years
